## Extra

> [!INFO] Euclidean Distance
> Euclidean Distance between two points is the shortest distance. 
> For example, distance between `(x1, y1)` and `(x2, y2)` is `Math.sqrt(Math.pow(x2-x1, 2) + Math.pow(y2-y1, 2))`.
> A smaller Euclidean distance means the points are more similar according to those features. It is the most common distance metric used in algorithms such as k-nearest neighbors (KNN) and k-means clustering.
